Who am I?

Hiya! I'm Julian Jaako Ahlberger and I'm a GUX and Game Designer located in Skövde, Sweden. My game development journey began back in 2018 when I was at first studying to become a 3D artist and Animator in high school, to then studying Game Design for my bachelor's degree and now going for a master's degree in Games User Experience.

Game Design

I’m a passionate about building intuitive, meaningful player experiences. As a Game Designer, I love experimenting with genres and mechanics to discover fresh ways players can interact and play.

GUX

My GUX focus is on onboarding and player retention, crafting clear and welcoming first-time experiences and thoughtful systems that keep players engaged over time because... Isn't that what gaming is about? I'm a bit of a digital product junky.

You can contact me here and/or check out my projects.
